GPUI Community Edition

A community fork of gpui, the fast, productive rust ui framework.

Try it:

gpui = { package = "gpui-ce", version = "*" }

The plan...

gpui-ce exists as a way for gpui contributors to explore what it would mean to take it further, without the constraints of supporting, or not breaking Zed.
